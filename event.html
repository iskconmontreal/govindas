Let Govinda’s Come to You

Govinda’s offers a vibrant and elegant space for hosting your special occasions — from birthday parties and corporate events to milestone celebrations. With its grand arches, curated lighting, and stunning art sculptures, the atmosphere sets the stage for a memorable experience.

We can comfortably accommodate gatherings of 10 guests and up (reservations required). For groups of 40 or more, please reach out to our event coordinator to arrange your booking.

Prefer to enjoy Govinda’s flavors off-site? Our catering team brings the Govinda’s experience to your venue, office, or home. Choose from our famous Indian dishes, tempting appetizers, crisp salads, hand-crafted flatbreads, and indulgent desserts — all tailored to fit your needs.
