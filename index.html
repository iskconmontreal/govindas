Montreal's Premier Vegetarian & Vegan Oasis.

Discover Govinda's, a vegetarian and vegan fusion restaurant nestled in a historic downtown Montreal building. We offer a beautiful, serene atmosphere featuring grand arches, unique lighting, and artistic sculptures – perfect for intimate meals or larger celebrations. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing an unforgettable experience with every visit.

At Govinda's, our mission is to craft fresh, nutritious plant-based meals that tantalize your taste buds, nourish your body, and bring peace to your mind. We promise an exceptional dining experience in a casual and tranquil setting.

Every dish is prepared using the freshest produce and finest ingredients, completely free of meat, fish, and eggs. Many of our options are also dairy-free, making them ideal for vegans.
